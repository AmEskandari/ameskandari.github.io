  • I am on the club fencing team at Dartmouth (DCFC). I have competed as a Men’s Epeeist in several regional competitions, as well as the 2017 club nationals competition in Lansing, Michigan, where we won second place for club fencing in the nation.
  • I am also a member of Dartmouth Formula Racing (DFR). I’ve worked on and driven both our electric and hybrid racecars. At some point, I’d like to give our electric car, Paula, self-driving capabilities.
  • I have been a member of the Dartmouth Finance Society since fall of my freshman year. I am currently the Vice President of the currencies group. I was previously Vice President of the equities group (Spring 2017), and Vice President of the fixed income group (Winter 2017).
  • When visiting home, I enjoy playing tennis and bridge with my childhood friends.
